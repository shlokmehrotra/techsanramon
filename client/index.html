Wait, but Why?

As you might already know, computer programming is an essential skill to learn throughout your life. Computer Programming is a big industry. Without it, computers, video games, the internet, and even cell phones would not exist. Jobs are not scarce for computer programming today. Almost everything we use has to do with technology, and computer programmers are needed in order to make the projects happen. Computer programming is one of the more challenging jobs out in the world. Someone who doesn't quit easily when things get hard, moves on to be a successful programmer.

By doing so, we are helping the future generation innovate new pieces of technology.
